Egg and Chips

Description

A deliciously simple meal that is cheap, filling and really easy to cook.
(serves 1)

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • Oven chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 200c
  2. Arrange chips on a baking tray and place in the oven for 20mins.
  3. When the chips are nearly cooked, heat some butter and vegetable oil in a frying pan.
  4. Crack the eggs into the hot oil and cook until the yolks are still slightly runny.
  5. Sprinkle some salt over the chips, some cracked pepper over the eggs and serve with a side of tomato ketchup.
