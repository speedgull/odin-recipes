Beans on Toast

Description

In a hurry? This tasty snack can be prepared in just a few minutes and is so simple that even a child can do it
(serves 2)

Ingredients

  • 1 tin of Branston baked beans
  • 4 slices of bread

Steps

  1. Put the bread in the toaster.
  2. Empty the beans into a bowl and cook in the microwave for 2min, stirring halfway through.
  3. Butter the toast.
  4. Pour the beans over the toast.
