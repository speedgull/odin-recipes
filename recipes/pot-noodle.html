Pot Noodle

Description

A favourite of lazy students. Quick and easy to prepare with no dishes to clean up afterwards
(serves 1)

Ingredients

  • Pot Noodle
  • Water

Steps

  1. Boil the water.
  2. Fill the pot noodle up to the fill line with boiling water.
  3. Replace the foil lid. Wait a few minutes and stir.
  4. Wait a few more minutes, add the sauce sachet and stir again.
